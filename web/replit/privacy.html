Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Thank you for your interest in LangGraph Dev Navigator. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store information when you use the waitlist and survey pages.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following data:

  • Email address and optional role from the waitlist form.
  • Survey responses, optional notes, and beta opt-in preference from the insight survey.
  • Operational metadata such as request timestamp and IP address for abuse prevention and service reliability.

2. Measurement and Analytics

The landing page emits product analytics events to the first-party endpoint /api/analytics when API forwarding is enabled.

Those first-party events may include event name, timestamp, page context, viewport details, and request metadata needed for funnel measurement.

We also use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) via Google tag (`gtag.js`) for website traffic and engagement reporting.

3. How We Use Data

  • Operate the waitlist and send project updates.
  • Prioritize roadmap decisions using aggregated survey demand.
  • Monitor reliability, detect abuse, and improve conversion flow quality.

4. Storage and Security

Waitlist entries, survey responses, and analytics events are stored in PostgreSQL through the project backend.

The service is deployed on Replit infrastructure and protected with standard transport security provided by the hosting platform.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Hosting: Replit (Replit Privacy Policy).
  • Analytics: Google Analytics 4 (Google Privacy Policy, How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services).

6. Data Retention and Requests

We keep waitlist and survey data while the program is active or as required for legitimate operational needs.

To request access, correction, or deletion related to your submitted email or survey data, contact: botingw_job@hotmail.com.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this page as implementation details evolve. Material changes are reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
